## Assignment 2
How to Make a Better egg Sandwich:
Get out one egg, 2 slice of bread, better, salt, and pan
Open the stove
Put the pan on the stove to get warm
Add little better and let it to melt
Crack the eggs into individual bowel
Add the eggs to the pan without bowel
Sprinkle the salt on the egg
Cook 2 to 3 min until the whites of egg are firm
Take the egg out of pan and put it in middle of slide of bread
Put both slices of bread together with eggs
Cut sandwich in half, if desirer
Put the bowel and pan in the sink
Put the better, salt and bread away
